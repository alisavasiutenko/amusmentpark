## ✨ Features
* 🎡 **Interactive 3D Rides:** Take a spin on the Ferris Wheel, ride the Merry-Go-Round, or experience the high-speed Rollercoaster!
* 🎯 **Playable Carnival Stands:** Test your aim at the Spit Toss and Shooting ranges.
* 🚶 **First-Person Walk Mode:** Explore the park on foot with WASD controls, spacebar jumping, and collision physics!
* 🌎 **Orbit Overview Mode:** View the entire park from above using dragging and zooming camera controls. 
* 📱 **Mobile Friendly:** Fully supports touch devices with an on-screen joystick and swipe-to-look camera.
* 🌃 **Beautiful Aesthetics:** Features a starry night sky, pulsing neon entrance sign, glowing track rails, and dynamic lighting.

## 🎮 How to Play
Just click here to play instantly: **[Visit the Amusement Park!](https://alisavasiutenko.github.io/amusmentpark/)**

**Walk Mode Controls:**
* **Move:** `W` `A` `S` `D` or Arrow Keys (Mobile: use on-screen Joystick)
* **Look:** Click & Drag mouse (Mobile: swipe on right side of screen)
* **Jump:** `Spacebar`
* **Interact:** Click or tap directly on any ride or stand while exploring!

**Orbit Mode Controls:**
* **Rotate Camera:** Click & Drag
* **Zoom:** Scroll Wheel
* **Interact:** Click directly on rides to view their info.
